<h2>📖 About</h2>

<p>
Waste2Grow is a sustainability-focused web platform designed to minimize food
waste by connecting waste generators with farmers and compost producers.

The platform leverages AI to provide smart waste classification, route
optimization, and efficient waste management workflows.
</p>
